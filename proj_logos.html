Northeast Iowa RC&D video intro

Animated intro I created using After Effects for Northeast Iowa RC&D. This intro was made by creating a 3D space and flying a virtual camera through Northeast Iowa RC&D images and videos. It ends by flying backwards through a stencil of the state of Iowa, and revealing the RC&D logo that I animated (see below).

Logos

The multicropping Iowa logo contains a soy bean plant and a wheat plant, representing a common crop combination used in multicropping practices. It also has a root system in the shape of Iowa, representing how multicropping keeps Iowa's soil healthy and prevents it from washing away. The Loonar logo represents the red eye of a common loon and a lunar/solar eclipse at the same time.
