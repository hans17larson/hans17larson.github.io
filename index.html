About Me

My name is Hans Larson, and I’m a Marketing Specialist at Eastern Carver County Schools. I recently returned to the Twin Cities to be closer to family, friends, and the hockey community I’ve become deeply involved in through officiating.

I grew up in Stillwater, Minnesota, and have always been passionate about creativity, technology, and community. I earned my bachelor’s degree in Data Science from Luther College, with minors in Computer Science and Visual Communication. During my time there, I developed a strong interest in Geographic Information Systems (GIS), which led to an internship at Northeast Iowa RC&D, where I worked on natural resource mapping and data visualization projects.

After graduation, I began my career as a Geospatial Analyst at Continental Mapping, where I created over 100 airfield maps for global defense projects. I later returned to Northeast Iowa RC&D in a full-time role that combined my technical and creative interests. As a Natural Resource Analyst & Marketing Tech, I contributed to a wide range of projects, including GIS and drone mapping, public outreach, video production, graphic design, web development, and grant writing. This unique position gave me the opportunity to explore multiple disciplines and grow as a versatile communicator and problem-solver. My work directing and animating the "Mike the Milk Jug & Friends" cartoon series earned the 2023 Excellence in Education Award from the Iowa Recycling Association and the 2022 Outstanding Interpretive Non-Print Media Award from the Iowa Conservation Education Coalition and Iowa Association of Naturalists—you can watch the series here.

Outside of full-time work, I’ve been an ice hockey official since 2020, officiating at high-level events like the NAPHL Showcase and the 2023 Central District Exposure Camp, where I had the chance to learn from Olympic and professional officials.

As the Marketing Specialist for Eastern Carver County Schools Community Education, I create marketing campaigns that help connect our community with programs for all ages. My work includes graphic design, photography, videography, animation, social media, print publications, digital content, and using data analysis to better understand our audience and improve outreach.

One of the projects I'm most proud of is developing Skylar the Stormhawk. What started as a simple logo became a recognizable mascot with a name, personality, and voice. I created animated videos, branded intros, and social media content that brought Skylar to life and helped make Community Education more fun, welcoming, and memorable.

I enjoy blending creativity with data to tell better stories, build stronger brands, and create experiences that people genuinely connect with.
